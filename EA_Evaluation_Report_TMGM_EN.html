III. Product One: GoldWave (AI Adaptive)

3.1 Product Positioning

GoldWave is an automated EA designed exclusively for XAUUSD (Gold) on MT5. Its defining feature is that the entry logic is not fixed. Instead of static indicator triggers, GoldWave uses AI-assisted adaptive decision logic to evaluate current market context in real time, then decides whether the moment is suitable to enter.

3.2 Core Logic

  • Evaluates whether the current risk environment is suitable
  • Evaluates whether market conditions are worth participating in
  • During uncertain or high-risk periods, the system remains patient and waits
  • Entry criteria adapt as market behavior changes — they are not locked to one rigid rule set

3.3 Risk Management

  • No Martingale
  • No Grid
  • No Averaging Down
  • Each trade is an independent decision with clearly defined risk control
  • A fixed stop loss exists as a last line of defense for extreme market conditions
  • Under normal conditions, exits are managed by the EA's internal logic well before the stop loss is reached

3.4 Trade Frequency Philosophy

The market moves every day, but not every day is worth trading. GoldWave prioritizes market quality over trade frequency. This disciplined restraint is key to controlling drawdowns and maintaining long-term stability.

3.5 Backtesting vs. Live Trading

GoldWave uses a news filter and dynamic risk control logic, parts of which rely on WebRequest — which cannot be fully reproduced in the Strategy Tester. Therefore:

  • Traditional backtests cannot fully replicate live behavior
  • We do not rely on highly optimized backtest curves as proof of reliability
  • Real, long-term forward performance provides far more meaningful reference

IV. Product Two: AXIO GOLD (Market Structure)

4.1 Product Positioning

AXIO GOLD EA is an automated trading system built specifically for XAUUSD (Gold) on MT5. Its core logic differs from most conventional gold EAs:

  • It is not a simple breakout system

  • It does not use rigid Buy Stop / Sell Stop placement logic

4.2 Core Logic

AXIO GOLD focuses on:

  • Market Structure
  • Support and resistance zones
  • The strength and quality of price reactions
  • Whether the surrounding market environment is suitable for participation

The system evaluates whether a setup is meaningful before taking action, rather than trading merely because a fixed technical signal appears — resulting in a far more selective and structure-aware entry behavior.

4.3 Risk Management

  • No Martingale
